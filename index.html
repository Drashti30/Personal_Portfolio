About Me

Hi, I'm Drashti Mehta.

I enjoy turning messy, complex data into clear insights that help people make better decisions. What started as a curiosity about finding patterns in data has grown into a passion for building intelligent systems that combine analytics, machine learning, and AI to solve real-world problems.

Over the past few years, I've worked on projects ranging from predictive analytics and business intelligence to large language models, healthcare AI, and forecasting. What excites me most isn't just building models—it's understanding the business problem first, asking the right questions, and creating solutions that people can actually use.

I enjoy working across the entire analytics lifecycle, from exploring raw data and engineering features to developing models, designing dashboards, and communicating insights to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. Every project is an opportunity to learn something new, whether it's a different industry, a new technology, or a better way to approach a problem.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring the latest developments in AI, experimenting with new tools, contributing to personal projects, and continuously expanding my skills. I believe the best data scientists combine technical expertise with curiosity, creativity, and a genuine desire to solve meaningful problems.

I'm always excited to collaborate on innovative work in Data Science, Machine Learning, Generative AI, and Analytics.
